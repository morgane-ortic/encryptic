ENCRYPTATOR 4000

The year is 2077. The world is in shambles.

We hacked important data from an oppressive mega-corporation. But now they're after us!

We need to store that info safely until things calm down. But how?

Luckily, we're hackers! We can just use our Encryptator 4000 to encrypt and store our data, and make sure that only we can access it.

Here's how we can do it.

First, let's register. We need to enter a user name and a password that will be stored to retrieve our data later.

We don't store the password directly, for safety reasons, but hash it and store only its hash. It's a form of cryptography that will enable us to check our password without having to store it in clear.

Then, let's enter messages containing our data. They will be directly encrypted to be inaccessible to anyone who doesn't know our password, and stored with their creation date + time.

F* you, megacorps!

Encryption worked! Our data is now safe. The corpo henchmen couldn't find anything!

But how can we retrieve it now?

We can log in with our username and password to access our new account on the cyber-darknet.

Note that if a corpo-bootlicker tries to log in without our password, they'll be locked out.

Once we're logged in we can see our messages again and when they were created. They will be imported from the data file and decrypted so we can read them.

We can also add another message to store with the others.

And if someone is getting too close to our crypto-server, we can delete either single messages or our account with all the messages it contains.

Alright, we're set up! Enjoy your privacy and be safe out there!
